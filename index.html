BIOGRAPHY

Oluwatomilayo Fasanmi is a Nigerian-born product designer. She grew up in the South-Western part of Nigeria. She has her bachelor degree in Microbiology at Obafemi Awolowo University, Ile-Ife.
She is a lover of God and a steward in God's Heritage of Faith Church. She is passionate about growth. She has worked as a senior product designer at the top tech companies in the world. She loves to see many young people leverage on technology to help solve the problems humans faces. She is happily married and her marriage is blessed with wonderful kids.
